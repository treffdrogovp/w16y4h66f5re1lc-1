<p><p>The gluten-free bread products market is categorized into two main types: no-gluten bread and little-gluten bread. No-gluten bread is entirely free from wheat and gluten-containing ingredients, catering to those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivities. Little-gluten bread, on the other hand, contains a small amount of gluten, often made with gluten-reduced grains, appealing to individuals who prefer a lower gluten intake but do not have severe allergies. Both segments aim to meet diverse dietary needs and preferences in today's health-conscious market.</p></p>
