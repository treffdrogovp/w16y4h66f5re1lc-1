Medium mineralized water, with 500-1500 mg/L, offers a balanced taste and beneficial minerals, attracting a broader audience. Highly mineralized water, exceeding 1500 mg/L, is favored for its therapeutic properties and distinct flavor, often targeting consumers with specific health interests. Each type serves varying hydration needs and preferences.</p></p>
