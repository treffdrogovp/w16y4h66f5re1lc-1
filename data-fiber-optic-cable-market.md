<p><strong>The Data Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data fiber optic cable market consists primarily of two types: single-mode and multimode cables. Single-mode fibers utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and large networks. In contrast, multimode fibers have multiple light paths, allowing for higher data rates over shorter distances, suitable for local area networks and data centers. Each type serves distinct applications based on distance, bandwidth, and installation requirements, catering to varying connectivity needs.</p></p>
